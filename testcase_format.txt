To insert N-triple/N-triple-star, write 3 lines

    line 1: 1
    line 2: graph_name
    line 3: n_triple_string

To insert print all statements of a graph, write 2 lines

    line 1: 2
    line 2: graph_name

To insert N-quad/N-quad-star, write 2 lines

    line 1: 3
    line 2: n_quad_string

To quit, write 1 line

    line 1: 0
